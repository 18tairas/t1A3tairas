In 2009 the Pittsburgh Penguins won the Stanley Cup beating the Detroit Red wings beating them 2-1 in Game 7.
From 2010-2012 Pittsburgh failed to go past the second round in the playoffs but during the 2013 season it all changed.
Pittsburgh Penguins stats during the 2013 season: GP 48/ Wins 36 / Losses 12/ Points 72.
During the first round they beat the Islanders 4-2 in a series of 6 games and in the second round they the Ottawa Senators 4-1 in a series of 5 games.
But in the third round the Pittsburgh Penguins lost 4-0 in a series of 4 games during the third round the lost to the Boston Bruins.
